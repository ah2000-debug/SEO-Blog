Welcome to KI-VO-Check, a daily blog by [Aegira](https://aegira.ai) covering the latest developments in AI governance and EU AI Act compliance.

## What to Expect

Every day, we publish concise, actionable updates on:

- **EU AI Act developments** — regulatory changes, enforcement timelines, and guidance from authorities
- **Compliance strategies** — practical approaches for mid-market European companies navigating AI regulation
- **Industry analysis** — how different sectors are adapting their AI practices to meet new requirements
- **Technical governance** — tools, frameworks, and architectures that support responsible AI adoption

The EU AI Act represents the most comprehensive AI regulation in the world. For European businesses, staying informed isn't optional — it's a competitive necessity. KI-VO-Check delivers the signal without the noise.
